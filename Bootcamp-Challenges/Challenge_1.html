History of Krave

Krave has been treating generations of customers since 1991. Our company was founded by Swedish native Gerald Krave who created the Krave bars. After being successful with his infamous Krave bars, Gerald went on to open a small retail treats shop where he sold various brand-name candy along with his signature Krave bars.
