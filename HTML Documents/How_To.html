How To Embed Video

Stack Overflow mentions that most youtube videos have an embed option which is found by pressing the share button under the video (or by right-clicking the video itself). To embed a Youtube video simply copy and paste the embed code from the video directly into html file. An example of this can be found here or by clicking the "HOME" link above.

Key Note About Embedding Youtube Videos

Sometimes when embedding youtube videos you may get an embedded Youtube placeholder but may have a message that says "video unavailable watch on youtube" or something similar. Popular videos such as music videos will not embed onto your page but there are some work arounds for this issue. Visit Stack Overflow for help fixing this issue.
